Campus Bot

Campus Bot is a chat bot that was born at IIT Delhi. Campus Bot is one stop solution for all the campus needs that students and the faculty in the campus can use. It has been integrated into numerous chatting platforms. Presently the bot has been trained for IITD but it can be later on trained for any campus by just modifying the format of the entry number i.e the Admission number.

Flash Subs

Flash subs is a python program that manages our media library i.e. it can automatically download subtitles and other information(Correct Title, IMDB rating, Plot Summary etc.) for movies and TV Series in our computer.

Subtitles are searched using- 1. Subdb database (hash) 2. Opensubtitles database (hash) 3. Opensubtitles database (name) 4. Bing (name) Thus, the probability of finding the subtitles become quite high.
